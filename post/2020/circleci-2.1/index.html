  • Manuel Schönlaub

CircleCI is a widely used and extremely flexible continuous integration tool. The new version, 2.1, was presented in July 2017.

This post is designed to give you a cue about the new features that enable DRY pipelines. I will focus mainly on the new concepts introduced in CircleCI 2.1. Understanding them is crucial if you want to proceed any further. Buckle up!
